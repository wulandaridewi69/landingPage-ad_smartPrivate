Feedback

At AD Smart Private, we greatly value all feedback from students and parents. Your input helps us continuously improve the quality of our teaching, customer service, and the overall learning experience. The scope of our feedback system includes the following aspects:

Teaching Assessment
  • Tutor quality: teaching ability, communication, and punctuality
  • The suitability of the learning method to the student's needs
  • Comfort and interaction during the learning process

Objective: To assess whether the tutor has provided effective teaching that meets expectations.

Service Satisfaction
  • Admin's response during consultation & registration
  • Ease of rescheduling or tutor replacement
  • Clear information regarding fees, schedules, and learning reports

Objective: To measure user satisfaction with the service in general.

Suggestions & Criticism
  • Input on areas that need improvement
  • Ideas or hopes from parents and students
  • Complaints that need to be followed up

Objective: To provide space for users to contribute to the development of our services.

Experience Testimonial

We also invite satisfied students and parents to share positive testimonials, either in writing or video, as part of our service promotion.

How to Provide Feedback
  • Through an online form after several lessons
  • Sending a direct message to the admin via WhatsApp
  • Answering routine surveys that we send periodically
  • Providing reviews on Google or our official social media
